Process

How I Work

01

Listen and Define

Every project starts with understanding the actual problem, not just the brief. I ask the questions most designers skip so I can design for the right outcome from day one.

02

Research and Structure

I look at what exists, what users actually do, and where the friction lives. Then I map out the information architecture and user flows before touching any visual tool.

03

Design with Intention

In Figma I work from wireframes to high fidelity without losing clarity. Every decision around spacing, type scale, color and interaction has a reason behind it that I can explain.

04

Build and Ship

For projects that need to go live fast, I use AI assisted tools like Cursor, Claude, Lovable and Framer to turn designs into real deployed websites. No waiting on a developer.

05

Iterate and Improve

Good design does not stop at launch. I stay involved through feedback cycles and make sure what ships keeps getting better over time.
