Andrestylez is more than a name — it's a philosophy. Born and raised in Lagos, his sound is a collision of Afrobeats, R&B, and street soul that refuses genre labels.

From late-night studio sessions to sold-out shows, every piece of music is a direct transmission from life lived fully — raw, styled, and always authentic.
