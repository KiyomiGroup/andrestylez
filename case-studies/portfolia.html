Project Overview

Portfolia is a portfolio builder designed specifically for creatives who want to show their work beautifully without dealing with complex tools or expensive subscriptions. The target user is a designer, illustrator, photographer or creative professional who has great work to show but no easy way to present it online in a way that does it justice.

The name itself says what the product does. It is your portfolio, made easy. The entire platform was built using vibe coding, allowing me to go from concept to a fully functional deployed product without a traditional development process.

The Problem

The options available to creatives who want a portfolio online sit at two frustrating extremes. On one end there are tools like Behance or Dribbble that are controlled environments where your work competes with everyone else's on the same platform. On the other end there are site builders like Webflow or WordPress that require real technical investment to do well.

Portfolia aims to sit in the middle. A tool that produces something that looks genuinely custom, feels personal to the creator and requires zero technical knowledge to set up. The creative fills in the blanks. The platform handles the rest.

Core Features

Project Gallery

Upload project images and organize them by category. The gallery layout adjusts automatically to present work in the best possible way.

About Section

A dedicated space to tell your story. Short bio, skills list and contact information all in one clean section that complements the work.

Custom Themes

Choose from a small set of carefully considered visual themes. Each one is designed to let the work be the focus rather than the template.

One Link Sharing

Every portfolio gets a clean shareable URL. One link to share in your bio, your email signature or your CV.

User Flow

Design Decisions

The setup flow was the most important design challenge. Every step needed to feel achievable. I kept each screen focused on a single decision so the user is never overwhelmed. The progress indicator at the top of each step makes the process feel finite and manageable.

The portfolio output itself was designed with the audience in mind as much as the creator. Recruiters and clients who view a Portfolia portfolio see clean, fast loading pages that focus entirely on the creative's work. No distracting UI. No platform branding competing for attention.

Vibe Coding Process

Building Portfolia was a great exercise in using AI for feature-dense UI. The gallery upload logic, the live theme preview and the link generation were all built through extended prompting sessions with Claude. I would describe the behavior I wanted, Claude would generate the implementation, and I would test, refine and improve the output until it matched the vision.

The gap between what I could describe and what Claude could build closed significantly as the session went on. The AI got better at understanding my conventions and preferences, and I got better at describing what I actually wanted with precision.

Outcome

Portfolia is live. It is a real tool that creatives can use to build and share their portfolios today. It demonstrates a design philosophy I believe in strongly: the best tools get out of the way and let the work speak. Portfolia does exactly that.
