Design that
communicates.

Graphic design is where I started. Before product design, before UI systems, before vibe coding, there were posters, logos and flyers. That foundation of visual communication thinking shows up in every other discipline I practice.

I work primarily in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and Figma. My graphic design output tends toward bold compositions, strong typographic choices and a color sense shaped by both global design culture and the visual energy of Lagos.

Whether it is a one off event flyer or a full brand identity system, the goal is always the same: make something that works visually before anyone reads the words.
